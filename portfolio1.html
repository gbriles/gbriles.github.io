Design

In order to establish the design parameters for the braking components, I created an excel calculator in conjunction with the designers of the brake pedal and master cylinder assembly in order to determine braking forces, among other figures. These guidelines allowed for the beginning of the design process, which involved the use of Siemens NX for CAD. During this process, me and the other member of the brakes team designed for optimization of strength-to-weight ratio of the components, as well as designing for manufacturability with 3-axis CNC equipment to reduce complexity. This yielded some of the lightest braking components in recent team history while maintaining strong components within the proper factors of safety.
